Tea Bag

Tea bags are an easy and convenient way to enjoy tea.

Here is the basic brewing method.

■ Cold Brew (Room Temperature)

Place one tea bag into 100ml of room-temperature water.

■ Brewing

Wait about 10 minutes.
Gently swirl the bag occasionally for even extraction.

■ Ready

Your tea is ready to enjoy.

■ Second Brew

You can enjoy a second infusion.
Use another 100ml of water and adjust the time to about 5–7 minutes.
