Hojicha

999 Hojicha is known for its warm roasted aroma.
Follow these steps to enjoy the best flavor.

■ Prepare the water

Hojicha is best brewed with high-temperature water.
Use about 90–95°C water.

■ Tea leaves

Use about 2g per cup (around 80ml).

■ Add leaves

Place the tea leaves in a teapot with enough room to expand.

■ Pour the water

Pour hot water directly and close the lid.

■ Steep

Wait about 30 seconds to 1 minute.
Do not over-steep to avoid bitterness.

■ Pouring

Pour evenly into cups and finish every last drop.

■ Second infusion

Use slightly hotter water and steep for about 20 seconds.

Enjoy the rich roasted aroma.
