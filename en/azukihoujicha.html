Azuki Hojicha

999 Azuki Hojicha has a roasted aroma with gentle sweetness.
Follow these steps for the best result.

■ Prepare the water

Boiling water is ideal for this tea.

■ Tea leaves

Use about 3g per cup (around 60ml).

■ Add leaves

Place the leaves in a teapot allowing room to expand.

■ Pour the water

Pour hot water directly and close the lid.

■ Steep

Wait about 1.5 to 2 minutes.
Avoid over-steeping to prevent bitterness.

■ Pouring

Pour evenly and finish every last drop.

■ Second infusion

Use hotter water and steep about 20 seconds.

Enjoy the sweet roasted aroma.
